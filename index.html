Research

I do not yet have a well-defined field of research. In the past, I have worked with optimal transport theory, autoencoders of various flavours, multi-modal language models, and manifold separability. I am also interested in, though generally uneducated about, homological alebgra, martingales and stochastic calculus, and exotic structures.
